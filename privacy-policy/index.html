File Finder

Privacy Policy

File Finder, a Shopify app that organizes users' files, is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure the security of that information.

Information We Collect:

File Finder collects personal information from users in order to provide the best possible file organization experience. The types of information we collect include:

  • Contact information such as name and email address
  • Information related to the files being organized, including file name, size, and type.

Use of Information:

File Finder uses the information collected from users to provide and improve our file organization services. This may include using the information to:

  • Provide customer support
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Analyze user behavior and preferences
  • Improve the quality of our services

Sharing of Information:

File Finder will not sell, rent, or share user's personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share user information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our services. These service providers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of user information and may only use it for the purpose of providing services to File Finder.

Security of Information:

File Finder takes the security of user information very seriously. We use industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access to, or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of user information.

However, no security measures are perfect and we cannot guarantee that user information will never be disclosed in a manner inconsistent with this privacy policy. We encourage users to use best security practices to help protect their information.

Access to Information:

Users can access, review, and update their personal information at any time by logging into their Shopify account. If a user needs assistance accessing or updating their information, they can contact us via email at support@dumfoundedtech.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy:

File Finder reserves the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the date at the bottom of this policy and, if necessary, by sending an email to the email address on file.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the practices of File Finder, please contact us via email at support@dumfoundedtech.com.
